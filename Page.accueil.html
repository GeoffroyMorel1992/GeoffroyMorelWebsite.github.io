About me:

Holding two Master's degrees in finance, one in banking finance and the other in finance, audit and controlling, I have gained over 7 years of experience as a financial controller and plant controller in international companies.

Convinced of the crucial importance of efficient data management for a company, I undertook a 6-month career break to deepen my skills in two areas:

  • Data analysis: I have trained myself in essential business software tools to exploit data in an optimal way

  • Financial analysis: Improving skills in data analysis, budgeting, forecasting and strategic recommendations

    • My ultimate goal was to acquire a dual specialization in data analysis and corporate finance.

    Project introduction:

    My project aims to leverage a comprehensive database containing data from an industrial company.

    Using this database, which I have stored on SQL Server, I have been able to:

  • Reconstruct the financial statements of the company

  • Implement financial and operational analyses

  • Establish a budget and forecasts

  • Make recommendations

    In order to conduct relevant analyses, it is essential to have extensive knowledge in accounting and finance, as well as advanced skills in the following areas:

  • General accounting (IFRS) and analytical accounting

  • Financial budgeting and forecasting (P&L, Balance Sheet and Cash Flow)

  • Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) in line with the role of Plant Controller

  • Advanced skills in SQL, Excel, VBA, Python and Power BI
